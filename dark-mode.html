The Philosophy of Craft

Every Detail,
Intentional

Our movements are not merely mechanisms. They are the culmination of a century of accumulated knowledge, held in the hands of those who care.

In-House Calibre

Our proprietary movements are developed entirely within our Geneva manufacture. 312 components, each toleranced to within 3 microns, assembled by a single watchmaker from start to finish.

Côtes de Genève Finish

Every visible surface of the movement receives hand-applied Geneva stripes — a decorative tradition that dates to the 18th century and requires a steady hand and years of practice to perfect.

Lifetime Covenant

Aurum pieces come with our Maison's perpetual service covenant. Not a warranty — a promise. Every service performed by the original artisan who assembled your watch, wherever possible.

Numbered Heritage

Each piece bears a unique number engraved on the caseback, registered in Aurum's perpetual archive. Your watch is not one of thousands — it is the only one of its exact story.

The Atelier Experience

From Hands
That Remember

Your Aurum begins as a conversation. Our artisans listen before they work.

01
Private Consultation
Speak with your dedicated atelier correspondent. Discuss proportions, metal, dial philosophy. There is no catalogue pressure — only possibility.
02
Movement Selection
Choose from three in-house calibres, each expressing time differently. A simple three-hand. A perpetual calendar. A minute repeater for those who wish to hear the hours.
03
The Making
Fourteen weeks in our Geneva workshops. You receive six progress reports. On completion, an invitation to witness the final regulation at the bench.
04
The Delivery
Presented in a hand-stitched calfskin box, accompanied by a letter from the artisan who built it — who they are, what they observed in your piece.
