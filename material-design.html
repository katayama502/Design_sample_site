How it works

From seed color to living UI

Pebble follows M3's structured approach — start with one color, end with a complete, accessible design system.

1

Choose a seed

Pick any brand color. Pebble's HCT color-space engine derives a full tonal palette — primary, secondary, tertiary, neutral, and error.

2

Set elevation roles

Assign surfaces to elevation tiers. Shadows and tonal overlays shift automatically, giving depth without manual tuning.

3

Apply shape tokens

Map your components to M3 shape scales — extra-small pill to extra-large card. Consistent corner radii across the whole product.

4

Ship & adapt

Export design tokens as CSS variables, Compose themes, or SwiftUI styles. Light and dark variants included out of the box.
